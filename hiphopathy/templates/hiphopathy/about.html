    Omoju Miller

    Omoju is the lead researcher on the "Hiphopathy" project between UC Berkeley and the International Computer Science Institute, which uses artificial intelligence techniques to deepen our understanding of human cognition and reasoning in hiphop lyrics. She has an undergraduate degree in Computer Science (2001) and a Master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering (2004) from the University of Memphis. She has over a decade of experience in the technology industry. She is currently a doctoral student at UC Berkeley in Computer Science Education. Omoju also serves in a volunteer capacity as an advisor to the White House Presidential Innovation Fellows, where she focuses on inclusion and diversity in technology.

    Alex Greenspan
