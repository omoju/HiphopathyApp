    In this Module you will learn what an algorithm is and a heuristic.

    Lets us first understand what it means to decode a sentence.

    Decoding means to translate from a code into the original language or form.

    For our purposes, decoding can be better understood as learning to translate from the literal understanding to the intended meaning of the rap poet. We will do this by understanding the context of the songs, learning to identify novel metaphors, and developing a process that we can use to get at richer meaning.

    Through our decoding of rap lyrics, what we will be doing is coming up with a set of general all-purpose instructions that we can follow, which will lead us in our translation of rap lyrics from their literal understanding to intended understanding. In computational thinking, this process is called the generation of an "algorithm".

    Now, lets us make our first attempt at decoding a rap lyric. Before we can do that we need to break the process down into sub components. In computational thinking, this process is called problem decomposition.

    Some Key words

    Algorithm
    A process or set of rules to be followed in calculations or other problem-solving operations.
    Heuristic
    A technique designed for solving a problem more quickly when classic methods are too slow, or for finding an approximate solution when classic methods fail to find any exact solution
    Abstraction process
    Deciding what details we need to highlight and what details we can ignore
    Decomposition
    A process by which a complex problem or system is broken down into parts that are easier to conceive and understand
    Conceptual Metaphor
    When one concept is understood in terms of another.
    (1) How can I kill a process?
    Here we use the language of killing to reason about a process. This is part of a class of metaphor that attributes anthropomorphic qualities on abstract entities.
    (2) Damn, that money we gave was just a Band-Aid.
    Here we use the language of healing a wound to reason about a societal problem. This is a JayZ lyric talking about aid after hurricane katrina. We could name this metaphor SocietalProblemIsPhysicalInjury metaphor.
