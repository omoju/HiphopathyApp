    In order to solve a problem, a Computer Scientist has to figure out the series of steps that can be followed which will lead to a solution. These series of steps is called an algorithm. Our first attempt at that process is coming up with a decoding algorithm. The table below is the results of our firs attempt.

    Decoding Algorithm
    Step Instruction
    1 Read the sentence(s) thoroughly
    2 Determine the context, if not given to you
    3 Identify metaphors (distinguish between literal and metaphorical language in text).
    Use the abstraction process to decide which words to focus on
    In our case, we will use an heuristic to focus our attention. Since one of the goals of this project is understanding how we reason about conceptual metaphors, we will use knowledge of conceptual metaphor research in natural language understanding to guide our solution1. In that field, they follow the heuristic of focusing on noun and verb phrases, with particular attention to action verbs.
