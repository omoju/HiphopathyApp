    Thinking like a Computer Scientist

    Computational thinking (CT) is is taking an approach to solving problems, designing systems and understanding human behaviour that draws on concepts fundamental to computing. Jeanette Wing.

    It essence is abstraction. What you will learn with the help of the hiphopathy tool is understanding abstraction and computational thinking in context

    What have Rap lyrics got to do with CT?

    One of the major goals of CT is to practice it in everyday context. For our purposes, we will use the decoding of rap lyrics to better understand the elements of computational thinking. Additionally, we shall see and learn the trips that these rap poets use to embed multiple meaning into their songs.

    Conceptual Metaphors

    One of the key competencies to develop when learning to think like a computer scientist is the development of rich abstractions. In the process of creating an abstraction, we have to decide what we need to highlight and what we can ignore. The conceptual metaphors in the rap lyrics will clue us in to the elements that we need to focus on.
